# Day 2 — Internship Log

**Date:** Jun 16, 2026  
**Project:** NIC College Ambassador Website  

## Today's Work

Today was focused on analyzing the collected requirements in detail, visualizing the complete system workflow, and defining how different components of the platform should interact. The goal was to transform the initial research findings into a structured system plan that could guide the development process.

## Things We Did

- Reviewed and refined the requirements identified during the research phase.
- Categorized requirements into functional and non-functional requirements.
- Analyzed features required for ambassadors, students, and administrators.
- Defined the expected workflow of users interacting with the platform.
- Visualized the complete system flow and user journey.
- Identified important modules required for the website.
- Analyzed authentication and authorization requirements.
- Planned user management and role-based access control.
- Studied the requirements for event management and ambassador activities.
- Analyzed communication and notification requirements.
- Identified database entities and possible relationships between them.
- Evaluated backend responsibilities and service requirements.
- Reviewed how different modules should communicate with each other.
- Considered scalability requirements for future feature expansion.
- Studied clean architecture principles for maintaining a structured codebase.
- Documented system behavior and technical decisions.
- Prepared the foundation for final architecture design.

## What I Learned

- How to convert business requirements into technical system components.
- The importance of visualizing workflows before implementation.
- How proper module separation improves maintainability.
- The role of database planning in building reliable applications.
- How clean architecture helps organize complex systems.
- Why defining user roles and permissions is important for secure applications.
- How requirement analysis influences architecture decisions.
- The importance of considering future scalability during system planning.

## Keynotes

By the end of the day, the requirements were analyzed in detail and the overall system workflow was visualized. Major modules, user interactions, and technical requirements were identified. The project moved from the research stage toward a structured architecture planning phase.
